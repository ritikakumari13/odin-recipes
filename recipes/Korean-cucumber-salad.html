Korean cucumber salad

Description

Korean cucumber salad, known as Oi Muchim (오이무침), is a refreshing and flavorful side dish that pairs perfectly with Korean BBQ or rice dishes. It features crisp cucumber slices tossed in a spicy, tangy, and slightly sweet dressing made with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and Korean red chili flakes (gochugaru). The sesame oil and sesame seeds add a nutty depth, making this dish irresistibly delicious.

This salad is quick to prepare and can be enjoyed immediately or after a short marination period to enhance the flavors. Whether you're looking for a light appetizer or a vibrant side dish, Oi Muchim is a fantastic choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber (or English cucumber), thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon Korean red chili flakes (gochugaru)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped scallion
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the cucumber: Thinly slice the cucumber and toss it with salt. Let it sit for about 15 minutes to draw out excess moisture.
  2. Drain and mix: Drain the cucumber slices without squeezing them, then mix them with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Adjust seasoning: Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  4. Serve: Enjoy immediately or let it sit for 10-15 minutes for the flavors to meld.
